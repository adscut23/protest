Vision & Mission
As teachers our core job is to help students to develop skills, create positive behaviours and innovate. With this in mind teachers academy is created with the vision to enable each teacher to become a world class educator. As a true patriotic of the Nation I owe this country the responsibility to nurture world class teachers so that we can be rest assured for the future we are creating for the next & many more generations. Teachers Academy’s vision is to empower teachers to be the catalyst of positive change maker in the society. Our world is at the mercy of great teachers and Teachers Academy is their go to place to ignite the spark of creativity.

We co-create with teachers that can exponentially increase teachers ability so that each lesson students learn becomes a life transforming lesson for them. We believe that if teachers see themselves as not teachers nut experience designers there be next level of engagement in the classrooms. And classrooms as theatres. Like all greatest teachers who have inspired the world. We want to demystify the skillset, mindset, environment set & tools and give access to every teachers so that they are able to imagine what they want to create and help them create that.

Inspire

Inspire how do we see the role of a teacher in the fourth industrial revolution where 3D printing, robotics, nanotechnology, Quantum computing, Artificial intelligence, Robotics, 5th generation wireless technology, Autonomous vehicle and decentralized systems all taking shape. How do we see the role of a teacher in such an advancement? the teacher Academy intend's to inspire and create the new metaphor for the 21st century teacher. It intends to transition the role of a teacher not just about teaching subjects and evaluating exams but as a true Pioneer in transforming a life of a student and making them into an amazing life. If you look at the history of the most beautiful teachers that ever walked on the planet like Maria Montessori, Aristotle, William McAfee,and slbm Jamie alyson's Edward p**** and many many more we would see that each of them had a certain skill set, mindset, environment set and tools that they invented and empowered and gave it to the world. The teacher Academy intense to inspire each one of us rather the entire humanity towards better education and better possibilities. If you want to build the nation we want to build our educators. Our teacher have the onus to pass the batton of regeneration, creativity and empowerment for the next generation. What Teachers Academy intends to do is transforming skill set, mind set,environment set, and tools into a powerhouse of transformation which is ever evolving and making this earth more positive place to live.


Co-create

The Teachers Academy intend's to shake hands with each one who are willing to co create the future that is around the development of skillsets, mindsets, environmentset & tools. We are committed to create an ecosystem of co-creation which is beyond the boundaries of any institution any country. Basically seeing the entire planet as one and co-creating massively with each individual across the globe. We are here to partner with you and to make our shared dreams come true let's go creative. Let's co-create.


Transform

Transformation is a journey of not possible to possible. The Teacher Academy is constantly discovering the gaps that are crying for Innovation. To transform the abilities of teachers and students to enable them to make happen what they thought not possible. We are committed to innovation for ever evolving journey of h elping students achieve their highest potential.
